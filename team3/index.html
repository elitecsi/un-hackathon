Pregnancy Test

Pregnancy tests are available in the Health Center. Students can meet with a nurse for testing.

Additional info

Emergency Contraception (Plan B)

Emergency Contraception (Plan B) is available in the Health Center. Plan B can be used up to 5 days after unprotected sex. Please be aware Emergency Contraception is more effective the sooner it is taken.

Additional info

Birth control consultations

Birth control consultations may be scheduled with a health care provider to discuss specific needs and types of contraception available.
